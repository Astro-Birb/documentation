<Tabs>
<Tab title="Config Daily Questions">
- **Channel:** Channel the QOTD is posted in. 
- **Ping:** The role that gets pinged when the QOTD is sent. 

> Daily Questions allow you to send daily questions to a channel with a ping to try and make your chat more active, so basically it's a chat reviver with a topic.

<Warning>Currently, the Daily Questions can be delayed by an Hour or even earlier.</Warning>
