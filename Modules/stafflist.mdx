---
title: Staff List
description: The staff list is a way off displaying all your staff on a automatically updated list.
---



<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Commands">
    - **/staff list send** - Sends the staff list in the channel its being used in.
    - **/staff list add** - Adds a role + its position on the staff list.
    - **/staff list remove** - Does the opposite.


  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
