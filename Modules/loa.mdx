> LOAs (Leave of Absence) allow your staff to temporarily step away from their duties without losing rank or permissions. This is useful for vacations, personal matters, or other commitments.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Options">
    - **LOA Channel**: Designate a specific channel for LOA notifications and updates.
    - **LOA Audit Channel**: Set a channel to log all LOA activity for record-keeping.
    - **LOA Role**: Assign a role to staff members while they are on LOA.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
