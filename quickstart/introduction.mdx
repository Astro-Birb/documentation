> **Astro Birb** is a Discord bot, built with powerful features to simplify your staff operations, moderation workflows
and empower growing communities with automation.

## What can I do with Astro Birb?
* Manage your staff with features like:
     * Infractions, complete with approval flows and automated actions
     * Tracking and logging of promotions
     * Automated tracking of staff performance and message quotas
* Collect structured staff feedback from your community on your staff team
* Manage LOAs from staff members with approvals and automatic processing
* Allow private communication between staff and members with tickets and modmail
* Automate repetitive tasks with custom commands and automated responses
* Automate the tedious task of maintaining a staff list

## How can I get started with Astro Birb?

Use the invite button at the top of this page to add the bot. If you have questions about how to use individual modules
or features, read through the relevant sections within this documentation. Additionally, you can join our
[support server](https://discord.gg/astrobirb) for help, updates, and community discussions. You can also help us by [reporting bugs](bughunting.mdx) or
suggesting new features on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/bugsbirb/birb).
