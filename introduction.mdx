> **Astro Birb** is a Discord management bot built to simplify staff operations, moderation workflows, and automation for growing communities.

Astro Birb helps server owners and administrators stay organized by centralizing tools like infractions, promotions, leave of absence tracking, feedback collection, and more!

### What you can do with Astro Birb

- Manage staff infractions with approval flows and custom actions
- Track promotions, quotas, and staff performance
- Allow staff to request LOAs without losing permissions
- Collect structured feedback from your community
- Automate routine moderation and management tasks

### Get started

If you're new to Astro Birb, we recommend you to read through each section of the documentation to familiarize yourself with its features and capabilities.

Additionally, you can join our [support server](https://discord.gg/astrobirb) for help, updates, and community discussions. You can also help us by [reporting bugs](bughunting.mdx) or suggesting new features on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/bugsbirb/birb).
