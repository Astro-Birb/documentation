Punish staff members using action workflows, Roblox integration and more.

Infractions allow you to manage your staff members more efficiently, which also integrates well with other established modules such as _Quota_ and _Staff Database_.

## How to void an infraction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Infractions Page">
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-10.png)
    </Frame>

    On the infractions page, hover over an infraction you want to void, and click.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Voiding it">
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-11.png)
    </Frame>

    Click the red button, it'll prompt you whether you want to void, say yes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to edit an infraction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Infractions Page">
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-13.png)
    </Frame>

    On the infractions page, hover over an infraction you want to edit, and click.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Editing the infraction">
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-15.png)
    </Frame>

    Click the edit button, the button that is being pointed at with 3 arrows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Prompt">
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-16.png)
    </Frame>

    Fill out the details that you want to change, and hit submit!
  </Step>
</Steps>
