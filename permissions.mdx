We have a permission system that relies on discord roles, it dictates what commands you can and cannot use.

## Setup Permissions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Most importantly, run /config.">
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-18.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Config Menu & Click on permissions.">
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-22.png)
    </Frame>

    Click on the permissions select option within in the Config Menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the staff role & admin role.">
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-23.png)
    </Frame>

    Select the staff role & admin role that fits the description. If you can't see the role on the dropdown list, remember you can search inside the dropdown box. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish">
    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-24.png)
    </Frame>

    Your permissions menu should look like this!
  </Step>
</Steps>
