---
title: Integrations
description: Learn how to integrate ERM Bot and Roblox Group into your Birb Bot!
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="How to enable ERM Bot Integration?">
   1. Run /config.
   2. Click on Config Menu and then go on 'Integrations'.
   3. Click Make Selection and click 'ERM'.
   4. Click Authorise button, when done click Done button.

<Steps>
  <Step title="How to enable Roblox Group Integration?">
   1. Run /integration link and link your Roblox account.
   2. Run /config.
   3. Click on Config Menu and then go on 'Integrations'.
   4. Click Make Selection and click 'Roblox Group'.
   5. Click Make Selection and click Group.
   6. Enter your Roblox Group ID.
