---
title: Integrations
description: Learn how to integrate Roblox Group into Astro Birb.
---

<Step title="Enable Roblox Group Integration">
   1. Run `/integrations link` > Select 'Roblox Group'.
   2. Run `/config`.
   3. Click on Config Menu and then go on 'Integrations'.
   4. Click Make Selection and click 'Roblox Group'.
   5. Click Make Selection and click Group.
   6. Enter your Roblox Group ID.
</Step>
