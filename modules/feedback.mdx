---
title: Staff Feedback
description: Collect feedback on your staff members
---

The **feedback** module allows your members to submit their feedback, on your staff members. You need to configure
a feedback channel where feedback is sent to, this can be public or private, for this module to function correctly.

## Configuration Options

<ParamField path="channel" type="channel" required>
    The channel to send staff feedback to
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="multiple feedback" type="boolean" default="false">
    Whether people should be able to provide feedback about the same staff member more than once
</ParamField>


## Commands
<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="/feedback give">
        Give feedback on a staff member's performance
        #### Parameters
        <ParamField path="staff" type="user" required>
            The staff member you are giving feedback on
        </ParamField>
        <ParamField path="rating" type="number" required>
            A rating from 1 to 10 for the staff member
        </ParamField>
        <ParamField path="feedback" type="string" required>
            Your feedback for the staff member
        </ParamField>
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="/feedback rating">
        View feedback that staff members have received
        #### Parameters
        <ParamField path="staff" type="user" required>
            The staff member you are viewing feedback for
        </ParamField>
        <ParamField path="scope" type="global/server" required>
            Whether to view their global feedback or just this server
        </ParamField>
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="/feedback remove">
        Remove feedback from a staff member
        #### Parameters
        <ParamField path="id" type="number" required>
            The ID of the feedback to remove
        </ParamField>
    </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
