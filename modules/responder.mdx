---
title: Auto Responder
description: Automatically responds to a response with a message of your choosing.
---

<Warning>
This feature is only available for [Premium](https://patreon.com/astrobirb) servers.
</Warning>

> The **Auto Responder** module allows you to set up automatic replies to specific keywords or phrases in your server.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Configuration Options">
    - **Trigger**: The keyword or phrase that will activate the response
    - **Response**: The message that will be sent when the trigger is detected
    - **Channel**: (Optional) Specify a channel where the auto-responder will be active
    - **Cooldown**: (Optional) Set a cooldown period to prevent spam
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
