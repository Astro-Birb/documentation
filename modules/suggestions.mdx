> The **Suggestions** module allows server members to submit ideas and feedback, helping you identify areas for improvement and new features for your server.

## Options

- **Suggestions Channel**  
  The channel where all submitted suggestions are posted.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Commands">
    - **/suggest**: Submit a suggestion  
      
      **Usage:**  
      `/suggest [suggestion] [image]`
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
