---
title: Suggestions
description: Collect suggestions from server members
---

The **suggestions** module allows your members to submit their ideas, feedback and suggestions for your server. You need
to configure a suggestions channel for your members to allow suggestions to be sent for you to review.

## Configuration Options

<ParamField path="channel" type="channel" required>
  The channel to send user suggestions to.
</ParamField>


## Commands
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="/suggest">
      Submit a suggestion to the configured suggestions channel for the server.
      #### Parameters
      <ParamField path="suggestion" type="string" required>
          The suggestion that you'd like to make.
      </ParamField>
      <ParamField path="image" type="image">
          An optional image can be attached to the suggestion
      </ParamField>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
